## Why Marriott Bonvoy

Bonvoy is one of the most flexible hotel programs, with a massive global footprint and useful airline transfer partners.

## Ways to Earn

- Co-branded credit cards with welcome bonuses
- 10x points at most Marriott brands, with bonuses for elites
- Promotions like double points and double elite nights

## Redemption Tactics

Focus on off-peak awards at aspirational properties, use the fifth-night-free benefit, and watch for transfer bonuses to partner airlines.
