## Program Overview

Marriott Bonvoy spans over 8,000 properties and dozens of brands, making it one of the easiest programs for consistent earnings.

## Fast-Track to Elite

- Aim for **Platinum Elite** via credit card welcome offers
- Use **double elite night** promotions to accelerate progress
- Stack paid stays with seasonal bonuses

## Redemption Sweet Spots

Look for off-peak pricing at resorts in Asia and weekend discounts in Europe. Combine **Free Night Certificates** with top-up points for aspirational stays.
