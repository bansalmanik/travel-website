## What is Marriott Bonvoy?

Marriott Bonvoy is one of the world's largest hotel loyalty programs, combining 30+ hotel brands under one umbrella. From budget-friendly Fairfield Inn to ultra-luxury St. Regis, Bonvoy offers incredible flexibility for travelers.

## How to Earn Marriott Bonvoy Points

### Staying at Hotels

The most straightforward way to earn points is through hotel stays:

- **Base earning:** 10 points per $1 spent
- **Silver Elite:** 10% bonus (11 points per $1)
- **Gold Elite:** 25% bonus (12.5 points per $1)
- **Platinum Elite:** 50% bonus (15 points per $1)
- **Titanium/Ambassador:** 75% bonus (17.5 points per $1)

### Credit Cards

Marriott co-branded credit cards offer accelerated earning:

- Earn 6x points at participating Marriott properties
- 3x points on dining and flights
- Automatic elite status with premium cards
- Annual free night certificates

### Promotions

Marriott regularly runs promotions offering bonus points:

- Double or triple points on stays
- Bonus points for multiple stays
- Category-specific bonuses
- Limited-time offers

## Best Redemption Strategies

### Sweet Spot Redemptions

Look for these excellent value redemptions:

1. **Category 5 properties** - Often 35,000 points for luxury hotels
2. **Off-peak pricing** - Save 20% on point redemptions
3. **Fifth night free** - Book 5 nights, pay for 4 with points
4. **Points + Cash** - Stretch your points further

### Peak vs. Off-Peak

Marriott uses dynamic pricing, but understanding patterns helps:

- **Off-peak:** Typically 20% fewer points required
- **Standard:** Regular pricing
- **Peak:** 20% more points during high-demand periods

## Common Questions

### Can I transfer Marriott points?
Yes, to airline partners, but the ratio isn't always favorable. Better to use for hotel stays.

### Do Marriott points expire?
Points expire after 24 months of inactivity. Any earning or redemption resets the clock.

### What's the best Marriott credit card?
Depends on your goals. Premium cards offer elite status and free nights, while no-fee cards provide solid earning rates.
