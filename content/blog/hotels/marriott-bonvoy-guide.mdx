## Earning Points

Focus on co-branded credit cards, partner dining, and booking direct to stack bonuses.

## Redeeming Smartly

- Target off-peak dates in popular cities
- Consider PointSavers promotions
- Move points to airline partners when award rates are high

## Elite Tips

Aim for Platinum for suite upgrades and lounge access. Use annual choice benefits for Suite Night Awards where available.
