## Why Marriott Bonvoy Matters

With over 8,000 properties worldwide, Marriott Bonvoy offers unmatched flexibility and value for frequent travelers.

## Earning Points Fast

- Leverage co-branded credit cards for welcome bonuses.
- Take advantage of PointSavers and fifth-night-free redemptions.
- Stack promotions during peak travel months.

## Redemption Sweet Spots

Look for off-peak pricing in Asia and Europe where points stretch furthest. Suite Night Awards can significantly improve your stay.

## Elite Status Tips

Aim for Platinum status to unlock free breakfast, lounge access, and guaranteed late checkout.
