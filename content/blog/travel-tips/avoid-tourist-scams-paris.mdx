## Common Scams

- **Petition Scam:** Ignore clipboards near major landmarks.
- **Friendship Bracelet:** Politely decline if someone grabs your wrist near Montmartre.
- **Metro Pickpockets:** Keep bags zipped and wallets in front pockets.

## Safety Checklist

1. Use contactless payments where possible.
2. Avoid setting bags on the ground at cafes.
3. Keep digital copies of passports and cards.

## What to Do If Targeted

Stay calm, walk away, and notify local authorities if you lose items.
