## Day 1: Shibuya and Shinjuku

Cross Shibuya Scramble, explore cat street cafes, then head to Shinjuku Gyoen for a calm afternoon.

## Day 2: Asakusa and Ueno

Visit Senso-ji before the crowds and sample street food along Nakamise-dori. Spend the evening museum hopping in Ueno.

## Day 3: Odaiba and Ginza

Ride the Yurikamome line for skyline views, shop futuristic malls in Odaiba, then wind down with omakase in Ginza.
