## Day 1: Arrival and Shibuya

Check into a hotel near Shibuya Station using Marriott or Hyatt points, then explore the famous crossing and Hachiko statue.

## Day 2: Culture and Views

Visit Senso-ji in Asakusa early to beat crowds, then head to Tokyo Skytree for panoramic views. Book a sushi omakase near Ginza for dinner.

## Day 3: Day Trips

Take the JR train to Kamakura or Nikko for temples and coastal walks before returning for a final ramen stop.
