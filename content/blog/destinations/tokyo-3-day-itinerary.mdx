## Day 1: Shibuya & Shinjuku

Start at Shibuya Crossing, explore Yoyogi Park, then finish with ramen near Omoide Yokocho.

## Day 2: Asakusa to Akihabara

Visit Senso-ji early, cruise the Sumida River, and shop for gadgets in Akihabara.

## Day 3: Odaiba & TeamLabs

Head to the bay area for TeamLabs, palette town views, and a soak in an onsen before flying home.
