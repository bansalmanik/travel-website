## Day 1: Shibuya & Shinjuku

- Start with coffee in Daikanyama before tackling Shibuya Crossing.
- Book evening bar seats in Golden Gai for izakaya hopping.

## Day 2: Asakusa & Ueno

Visit Senso-ji before the crowds, then stroll along the Sumida River and finish with ramen in Akihabara.

## Day 3: Day Trip

Take the early train to Hakone for hot springs and Mt. Fuji views. Reserve Romancecar seats in advance to avoid queues.
