## Snapshot

The HDFC Regalia remains a well-rounded mid-tier card thanks to its lounge access and consistent reward earning.

## Standout Benefits

- Complimentary domestic and international lounge visits
- Milestone bonus on annual spends
- Access to concierge and travel assistance services

## Verdict

Keep the card if you maximize SmartBuy offers and frequently use lounges. Otherwise, consider upgrading to premium options.
