## Why the Regalia Still Matters

The Regalia line remains a reliable mid-tier card thanks to wide lounge acceptance and predictable rewards.

## Benefit Highlights

- **Lounge access** at domestic and international terminals
- Milestone benefits that offset the annual fee
- Extensive merchant offers on dining and travel

## Redemption Tips

Pair the card with SmartBuy for accelerated earnings and transfer points to airline partners during bonus windows.
