## Program Overview

Air India modernized its frequent flyer program with clearer tier names and better earning on international routes.

## Easy Ways to Earn

- Flights on Air India and Star Alliance partners
- Credit card transfers from major bank currencies
- Bonus promotions for new routes

## Where to Redeem

Look for business-class awards to Europe and North America, plus upgrades on dense domestic sectors when fares are low.
