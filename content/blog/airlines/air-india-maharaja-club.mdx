## Qualification Paths

- Book higher fare buckets on key routes to accumulate status points faster
- Use Star Alliance partner flights to close gaps
- Track progress in the AI mobile app to avoid surprises

## Upgrade Strategy

Redeem upgrade instruments on long-haul flights first where the value per point is highest. Avoid peak holiday periods to improve odds.
